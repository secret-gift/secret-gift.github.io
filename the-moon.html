Every time I look at the moon,
I think of you.
Covered in spots, Oh so black!
Reminding me of all the manners you lack.
How your words brought me the darkest of nights
Yet your love made me happy and bright!
No matter how many flaws,
But on the edges it glows.
Everytime I look at the moon,
I think of you.
Everytime I look at the moon,
I still miss you...

No matter how dark the night gets, keep glowing, keep growing.
